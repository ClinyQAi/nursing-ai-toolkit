The environmental cost of GenAI is often invisible but significant. As nursing professionals committed to health and wellbeing, understanding and mitigating these environmental impacts is part of our professional responsibility.

## Mitigation Strategies

  <div className="mdx-card">
    <span className="mdx-icon">ğŸ‘©â€ğŸ«</span>
    <h3>For Educators</h3>
    
    <h4>Conscious AI Use</h4>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Evaluate Necessity:</strong> Ask "Is AI the best tool?" before using.</li>
      <li><strong>Efficient Tools:</strong> Use smaller models or text-only for simple tasks.</li>
      <li><strong>Optimize:</strong> Craft effective prompts to avoid retries. Archive and reuse responses.</li>
    </ul>

    <h4>Institutional Actions</h4>
    <ul>
      <li>Select green providers.</li>
      <li>Monitor AI-related emissions.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div className="mdx-card">
    <span className="mdx-icon">ğŸ“</span>
    <h3>For Students</h3>
    
    <h4>Mindful Consumption</h4>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Reduce:</strong> Don't use AI for tasks you can easily do yourself.</li>
      <li><strong>Optimize:</strong> Plan prompts to be specific and reduce iterations.</li>
      <li><strong>Stewardship:</strong> Maintain devices to extend their lifespan.</li>
    </ul>

Understanding the costs of AI is important, but what does this mean in practice? This page translates theory into actionable guidance for every role in nursing education.

## For Educators

<div className="mdx-grid-2">
  <div className="mdx-card">
    <span className="mdx-icon">üìù</span>
    <h3>Course Design</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>AI-Aware:</strong> Explicitly address AI in course outlines. Define "acceptable" vs "unacceptable" use.</li>
      <li><strong>Assessment:</strong> Focus on <em>process</em> (reflections, oral defense) over product. Include AI-free components.</li>
    </ul>
    <details>
      <summary><strong>Example Module Structure</strong></summary>
      <p><strong>Wk 1-2:</strong> Intro & Ethics</p>
      <p><strong>Wk 3-4:</strong> Critical Evaluation (AI vs Evidence)</p>
      <p><strong>Wk 5-6:</strong> Practical App (Care Planning with oversight)</p>
      <p><strong>Wk 7-8:</strong> AI-Free Assessment (Simulation/Viva)</p>
    </details>
  </div>

  <div className="mdx-card">

    <ul>
      <li><strong>Model Transparency:</strong> Show your own AI use‚Äîsuccesses <em>and</em> failures.</li>
      <li><strong>Structured Activities:</strong> Run "AI vs Traditional" comparative exercises.</li>
      <li><strong>Discussion:</strong> Ask "What would you have done differently without AI?"</li>
    </ul>

:::tip Policy Development
**Institutional Guidelines** must be clear. Consider a "Traffic Light" system:
*   **Green:** Generating ideas, communication practice.
*   **Amber (Disclose):** Drafting text, literature searching.
*   **Red (Prohibited):** Patient data entry, closed-book exams.
:::

## For Students

<div className="mdx-grid-2">
  <div className="mdx-card">
    <span className="mdx-icon">üéì</span>
    <h3>Daily Practice Protocol</h3>
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Try First:</strong> Attempt the task independently. Use AI only for specific blocks.</li>
      <li><strong>Stay Critical:</strong> Question every response. Verify against NICE/BNF guidelines.</li>
      <li><strong>Document:</strong> Save your prompts. Be ready to explain <em>how</em> you used the tool.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>

  <div className="mdx-card">
    <span className="mdx-icon">üìö</span>
    <h3>Study Strategies</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Balanced Diet:</strong> Limit AI to max 20% of study time.</li>
      <li><strong>Active Learning:</strong> Use AI to generate practice questions or flashcards, then verify them.</li>
      <li><strong>Retention:</strong> Handwrite notes from primary sources to cement memory.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

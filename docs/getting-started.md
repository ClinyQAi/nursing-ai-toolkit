# Getting Started

Welcome to the **AI Educator Toolkit**, a practical guide for integrating Generative AI into UK nursing education. This toolkit is designed to help educators, students, and institutional leaders navigate the rapidly evolving AI landscape safely and effectively.

## üõ†Ô∏è Core Principles

Every resource in this toolkit is grounded in three core principles:

1.  **Person-Centred**: AI should enhance, not replace, the therapeutic nurse-patient relationship.
2.  **Human-in-the-Loop**: Clinical decisions must always be made and verified by a human professional.
3.  **Aligned with Standards**: All guidance is mapped to the **NMC Future Nurse (2018)** proficiencies.

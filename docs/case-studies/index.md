## Why Case Studies?

Case studies bridge the gap between AI theory and clinical reality. They help educators:
1. **Reduce Anxiety**: See how others have successfully (and safely) used AI.
2. **Standardize Practice**: Align AI use with institutional and professional standards.
3. **Inspire Students**: Show them how to use AI for professional development, not just task completion.

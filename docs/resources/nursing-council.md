# âš–ï¸ Nursing Council Agent

The **Nursing Council Agent** is an innovative AI tool designed to simulate a professional peer review panel. It leverages the "**Council of Models**" approach to provide nursing educators with comprehensive, balanced feedback on their lesson plans, clinical scenarios, or curriculum materials.

Instead of a single AI response, the Council convenes three distinct "experts" to review your work from different angles.

:::info Aligned with UK Standards
The agent's personas are specifically tuned to align with **UK Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) Standards** and person-centred care principles.
:::

## üß© The Logic Behind the Magic

These templates use the **C.R.E.A.T.E.** framework to ensure consistent results:
-   **C**ontext: "I am a nursing student..."
-   **R**ole: "Act as a Clinical Examiner..."
-   **E**xpectation: "Ask me one question at a time..."
-   **A**udience: "Explain as if I am a novice..."
-   **T**one: "Conversational, no jargon..."
-   **E**vidence: "Based on UK guidelines..."

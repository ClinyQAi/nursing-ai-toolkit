## What is This?

The **Nursing Proficiencies+ Coach** is an interactive AI chatbot designed to support nursing students in learning and understanding nursing proficiencies. Built on the fine-tuned MedGemma model, it provides guided, pedagogical support using a Socratic teaching approach.

## How It Works

The coach uses a **Socratic teaching method** to:
- Ask leading questions rather than providing immediate answers
- Scaffold learning based on your year and nursing field
- Encourage critical thinking and self-reflection
- Provide hints and guidance when you're stuck
- Celebrate your progress with encouragement
